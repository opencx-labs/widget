# Refunds

You can refund a payment fully or partially. Refunds always go back to the
customer's original payment method.

## How to issue a refund

1. Open the payment (Payments → click the row).
2. Click **Refund**.
3. Enter the amount — it defaults to the full refundable balance. Enter a smaller
   amount for a partial refund.
4. Optionally add a reason, then confirm.

The assistant can also do this for you — just ask, e.g. *"refund payment
tr_0020264795"* or *"refund €5 on the last Gift card order."*

## Rules

- Only **paid** or **partially refunded** payments can be refunded.
- You can't refund more than the remaining (unrefunded) amount.
- Refunding the full remaining amount sets the payment to **refunded**; a smaller
  amount sets it to **partially refunded**.
- Refunds start as **pending** and settle to **refunded** once processed.

## Timing

Most refunds reach the customer in 1–3 business days, though card refunds can take
up to 5–10 business days depending on their bank. The money is deducted from your
next settlement.
