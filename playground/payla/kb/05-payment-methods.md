# Payment methods

Payla supports the methods your European customers expect:

- **iDEAL** — the most popular online method in the Netherlands. Bank-to-bank,
  confirmed in seconds, and cannot be charged back (low fraud risk).
- **Cards** — Visa, Mastercard and more. Can be disputed/charged back.
- **PayPal** — familiar wallet checkout.
- **Bancontact** — the leading method in Belgium.
- **Apple Pay** — fast wallet checkout on Apple devices.
- **Klarna** — buy now, pay later.
- **SOFORT** — bank transfer method used in Germany and Austria.
- **Bank transfer** — manual transfers; slower to confirm (stays *pending* until
  the funds arrive).

## Which methods show at checkout?

Customers see the methods enabled for your account and relevant to their country
and device (for example, Apple Pay only appears on Apple devices).

## Chargeback risk by method

Bank-based methods (iDEAL, Bancontact, bank transfer) generally can't be charged
back. Cards and wallets can, so keep clear descriptions and fast fulfilment to
reduce disputes.
