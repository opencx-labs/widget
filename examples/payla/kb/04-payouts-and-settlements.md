# Payouts and settlements

A **settlement** is a batch of payments paid out to your bank account together.

## Your balance

- **Available** — cleared money, ready to be paid out.
- **Pending / on the way** — recent payments still clearing before they become
  available.

You can see both on the **Balances** page.

## Settlement statuses

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Open** | The current batch, still collecting payments. |
| **Pending** | Closed and queued for payout. |
| **Paid out** | Sent to your bank account. |
| **Failed** | The payout couldn't be completed (check your bank details). |

## Payout schedule

By default Payla pays out **daily**. You can change this to weekly or monthly in
Settings. Refunds and chargebacks are deducted from the settlement they fall in,
so a settlement amount is *net* of any money returned.

## Common questions

- **"When will I get paid?"** Available balance is paid out on your schedule
  (daily by default). Pending money is paid once it clears.
- **"Why is my settlement smaller than my sales?"** Refunds, chargebacks and fees
  for that period are netted out of the settlement.
