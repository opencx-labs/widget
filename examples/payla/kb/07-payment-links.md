# Payment links

A **payment link** lets you charge a customer without any code or integration.
You create a link for a fixed amount and share it — by email, chat, or invoice —
and the customer pays on a hosted checkout page.

## Creating a link

1. Go to **Payment links → New payment link**.
2. Enter a description and amount.
3. Share the generated URL.

The assistant can create one for you — e.g. *"create a payment link for €49.99
for a wholesale order."*

## Link statuses

- **Active** — live and payable.
- **Paid** — the customer has paid; the link is closed.
- **Expired** — no longer payable.

## Good uses

- Invoices and deposits.
- One-off or custom charges.
- Selling before your website integration is ready.

When a link is paid, it produces a normal payment that appears on your Payments
page and settles like any other.
