# Getting started with Payla

Payla is a payments platform for growing businesses. From your dashboard you can
accept payments, refund customers, track payouts, manage customers, and create
shareable payment links.

## The dashboard at a glance

- **Home** — your balance, 30-day revenue, success rate and recent activity.
- **Payments** — every transaction, filterable by status and method.
- **Payment links** — shareable links to charge customers without an integration.
- **Customers** — everyone who has paid you, with lifetime spend.
- **Balances** — money available now and money still clearing.
- **Settlements** — the payout batches sent to your bank account.
- **Disputes** — chargebacks that need your response.

## Test data vs live

New accounts start in **test mode** so you can explore safely. The "Test data"
badge in the top bar tells you what you're looking at. Switch to live once your
account is verified.

## Getting help

Use the assistant in the bottom-right corner any time. It can look up a specific
payment, explain a status, check your balance, issue a refund, or create a
payment link for you.
